About the Advancing World Missions Conference (AWMC)

The Advancing World Missions Conference is a pivotal event for pastors, mission leaders, and practitioners across Africa. Keynotes Sessions

Experience powerful messages from renowned mission leaders, igniting your passion and vision for global evangelism. These sessions will inspire and challenge you to deepen your commitment to reaching the nations.

Breakout Sessions

Dive deeper into specific mission topics, from cross-cultural communication to church planting strategies. Choose from a variety of focused discussions designed to equip you with practical insights.

Training Workshops

Develop essential skills for effective mission work through interactive, hands-on sessions. Learn practical tools and methods to enhance your ministry impact and overcome common challenges.

Networking Opportunities

Connect with fellow pastors, mission leaders, and practitioners from across Africa and beyond. Forge strategic partnerships and share experiences to strengthen the collective effort of global missions.

Questions and Answers

What do I stand to gain from the conference?

At this conference, you'll have the opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals who are passionate about advancing global missions, discover innovative approaches to spreading the gospel, learn about effective mission strategies, and gain practical tools to overcome challenges in the field. From engaging workshops and breakout sessions to thought-provoking panel discussions and exhibitions, our lineup of esteemed speakers will share their experiences, insights, and strategies for making a lasting impact in global missions. Whether you're a seasoned missionary, a church leader, or simply curious about missions, this conference is for you.

When and where is the conference being held?

The conference will be held from August 13-17, 2025, at the Conference Center for the Nations, 4 Parliament Highway, Opposite Oyo state House of Assembly, By Federal INEC, Secretariat, Ibadan.

How do I register for the conference?

Although there are arrangements for physical registration at the conference venue, every intending participant is expected to register online via this link: Register This is to better help us plan and prepare for conference attendees.

Can I participate virtually?

Yes, you can participate online. The event will be broadcast online via YouTube, Facebook and other social media platforms. You can also download conference materials from the website to follow along.

What arrangements for physical attendees?

Adequate transport arrangement has been put in place for safe and easy commuting of all conference participants. Food vendors will also be around to sell to you at reasonable prices. General lodging arrangements have been put in place. Should you however decide to make personal lodging arrangements, see Hotel Booking Information for more.

Is there any arrangement for children and teens?

Yes, there is. Our History Makers Church will be holding 'Children in Missions', a special event for kids and teenagers during the course of the conference. Adequate security arrangements have been made to ensure the safety of everyone at the event.

I would like to give to this initiative. How do I go about it?

AWMC is non-profit status. In order to make the conference available and affordable to every member of the family of Christ, no registration fee is required. It is financed in four (4) ways: Donations from sponsoring bodies, Display space for missions exhibitions, Offerings given at the conference, and Adverts supporting missions. We operate a modest budget and our accounts are presented each year. You are also invited to share in the financial support of AWMC by making donations to our bank account: FMI - Advancing World Missions Conference (1013901151, Zenith Bank PLC Bodija Ibadan).
