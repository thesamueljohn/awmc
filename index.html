THE UNREACHED POPULATION

0

increasing by 2.5 people every second

Out of 8 billion people on Earth, over 3.5 billion have never once heard the name of Jesus.

Every single second, 2.5 people pass away... many of them without ever hearing the Gospel.

That is 150 people every minute. 9,000 every hour. 216,000 every single day.

THE REGION

The 10/40 Window

A geographic band stretching from North Africa to East Asia, between 10 and 40 degrees north latitude.

THE PEOPLES

The Major Blocs

Home to the vast majority of the world's Muslims, Hindus, and Buddhists, residing in thousands of distinct ethnic groups.

THE INEQUALITY

Resource Imbalance

Over 90% of all unreached peoples live here, yet they receive less than 1% of global missionary resources and funding.

THE REALITY

Absolute Isolation

Millions are born and die without ever meeting a single Christian, having a Bible in their language, or seeing a church.

CONFERENCE FAQ

Questions & Answers

Find clear answers to help you prepare, register, and plan for your attendance at AWMC 2026.

What do I stand to gain from the conference?
At this conference, you'll have the opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals who are passionate about advancing global missions, discover innovative approaches to spreading the gospel, learn about effective mission strategies, and gain practical tools to overcome challenges in the field. From engaging workshops and breakout sessions to thought-provoking panel discussions and exhibitions, our lineup of esteemed speakers will share their experiences, insights, and strategies for making a lasting impact in global missions. Whether you're a seasoned missionary, a church leader, or simply curious about missions, this conference is for you.
When and where is the conference being held?
The conference will be held from August 12-16, 2026, at the Conference Center for the Nations, 4 Parliament Highway, Opposite Oyo state House of Assembly, By Federal INEC, Secretariat, Ibadan, Nigeria.
How do I register for the conference?
Although there are arrangements for physical registration at the conference venue, every intending participant is expected to register online via the registration form. This is to better help us plan and prepare for conference attendees.
Can I participate virtually?
Yes, you can participate online. The event will be broadcast live via YouTube, Facebook, and other social media platforms. You can also download conference materials from the website to follow along.
What are the lodging and lodging arrangements for physical attendees?
Adequate transport arrangement has been put in place for safe and easy commuting of all conference participants. Food vendors will also be around to sell meals at reasonable prices. General lodging arrangements have been put in place. Should you decide to make personal lodging arrangements, see Hotel Booking Information for more.
Is there any arrangement for children and teens?
Yes, there is. Our History Makers Church will be holding 'Children in Missions', a special event for kids and teenagers during the course of the conference. Adequate security arrangements have been made to ensure the safety of everyone at the event.
I would like to give to this initiative. How do I go about it?
AWMC has a non-profit status. In order to make the conference available and affordable to every member of the family of Christ, no registration fee is required. It is financed through: Donations from sponsoring bodies, display spaces for missions exhibitions, offerings given at the conference, and adverts supporting missions. You are invited to share in the financial support of AWMC by making donations to our bank account: FMI - Advancing World Missions Conference (1013901151, Zenith Bank PLC Bodija Ibadan).
How can I get more information or ask further questions?
